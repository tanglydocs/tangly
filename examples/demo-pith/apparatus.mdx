# Apparatus

The handbook leans on a few small mechanisms. Each is in service of the
prose, not the other way round.

## Keyboard shortcuts

Press <Kbd keys="Cmd+K" /> from anywhere to search. Inline keys also render
nicely: <Kbd>Esc</Kbd> closes a modal; <Kbd>Tab</Kbd> moves focus.
